Pitch

Whole Foods Bundle

We are proposing an online delivery service, within the amazon platform, which curates affordable and nutritional focused bundles, selected by Whole Foods, for users located within food deserts; in-order to improve their accessibility to fresh, organic, produce while presenting it in an approachable way.

Business Problem

Whole Foods

In early 2017, Whole Foods began to see a decrease in their quarterly profit margin due to market saturation in affluent neighborhoods.

In order to reverse the trend in decreasing profit margins, Whole Foods is now attempting to attract a new set of consumers, within food deserts. However, they are currently facing three frictions as they are viewed, outside of the aspirational class, as unaffordable, inaccessible, and unapproachable

Business Opportunity

Amazon

On June 15 2017, Whole Foods Market officially became a subsidiary of Amazon after being purchased by the tech company for 13.7 billion dollars.

Amazon’s acquisition of Whole Foods has solved the friction of unaffordability, through their reduced prices, and has created a newfound opportunity to leverage Amazon’s industry leading digital channel infrastructure to make Whole Foods more accessible to a wider demographic and more approachable to people who traditionally viewed organic food as a luxury.

Frame

Intervention

The main two frictions that we can address is how to improve accessibility and approachability when it comes to Whole Foods.

From our previous insights we formed our opening to be :

How might we utilize Amazon’s accessibility and digital channels to help Whole Foods improve the brand’s approachability in lower income markets, in order to expand beyond their core demographic of the “aspirational class” ?

From this, our goal is to utilize the strength from both of these companies, through a digital touchpoint, to better serve those within food deserts.

Solution

Online Delivery Service

Whole Foods Bundle curates affordable and nutritional focused bundles for users, within food deserts, to order via delivery or pickup.

The implementation of Whole Foods bundle, within Amazon’s digital infrastructure, increases the accessibility of receiving nutritional, organic, produce to those that are confined within food deserts.

Reflection

Personal Insights

I found this project interesting as the Whole Foods and Amazon merger is quite recent so there weren’t many constraints when making a proposal. What I enjoyed most about this project was that we got to utilize the strengths of both brands to better accommodate a neglected demographic. My goal in the future is to cater more design projects towards a situation or environment where I can help people in need.
