Pitch

Quiksilver Reach

We are proposing an athletic performance digital application called Quiksilver Reach made for aspiring surfers.

Reach is designed to visualize performance insights by identifying patterns in your surf sessions to make improving your technique easier. Through this implementation, our goal is to bring Quiksilver back into the forefront of the surfing community.

Business Problem

Quiksilver

Quiksilver is a well known brand, however they declared bankruptcy in 2015. We wanted to investigate what lead to this outcome and how we could intervene to help.

Quiksilver is an American retail sporting company based in California, it’s one of the largest manufacturers for surfing and boardsport equipment. Their goal is to bring authenticity and passion to board sports for young athletes.

“Quiksilver diverged from the needs and wants of its core customer, the athlete” -(Carroll, 2015).
